## Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Zuan ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to ensuring that your data remains private and secure.

---

## Data Collection and Processing

### On-Device Processing

Zuan performs all AI transcription and audio processing entirely on your device. **No audio data, transcripts, or personal content is sent to external servers for processing.** Your content stays on your device at all times.

### Data We Do Not Collect

- Audio recordings or files
- Transcript content
- Listening history
- Personal messages or communications
- Location data
- Contact information
- Browsing history

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **Podcast subscriptions and episode metadata** — stored on-device and synced across your devices via Apple iCloud (CloudKit).
- **Downloaded podcast episodes** — stored in your device's local storage.
- **AI-generated transcripts** — stored locally alongside cached media.
- **Playback positions and preferences** — stored locally using standard iOS storage mechanisms.
- **Imported audio files** — stored in the app's local Documents directory.

### iCloud Sync

Zuan uses Apple's CloudKit to sync podcast subscriptions across your devices. This data is managed by Apple and is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not operate any intermediate servers for this sync.

---

## Third-Party Services

### Podcast Search

Zuan uses the iTunes Search API and/or Podcast Index API to search for podcasts. When you search for a podcast, your search query is sent to these services to return results. No personal information is included in these requests.

### Advertising

Zuan uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). This may include:

- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region level)
- Ad interaction data

You can control personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings:

1. Go to **Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking** on your iOS device.
2. Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" to limit ad personalization.

Zuan complies with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and will request your consent before enabling personalized ads.

### Analytics

Zuan uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data that helps us improve the app. This data is **not linked to your identity** and includes:

- Feature usage events (e.g., playback started, podcast subscribed, media imported)
- App session information (e.g., session duration, app version)
- General device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, country)

Firebase Analytics does **not** collect:

- Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- Content of your transcripts or audio
- Specific listening history or playback content

This data is processed by Google and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can learn more about how Google uses this data at [Google's partner site policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### RSS Feeds

When you subscribe to a podcast, Zuan fetches the podcast's RSS feed directly from the publisher's server. This is a standard HTTP request and may expose your IP address to the podcast host, as with any podcast app.

---

## Data Retention

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data:

- **Delete app data** by uninstalling the app.
- **Delete individual items** (transcripts, downloads, library items) within the app.
- **Clear all cached data** using the storage management feature in Settings.

## Children's Privacy

Zuan does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal data.
